About me

Background & Biography

My interest in technology began at a very young age. I was first introduced to computers as a child, and over time this exposure developed into a strong curiosity about how software systems work. While many people use computers simply as tools, I wanted to understand how everything running behind the screen was created. After graduating high school in 2023, I chose to pursue Computer Engineering at Epicode Institute of Technology in Milan, Italy. This decision was driven by my interest in combining logic, problem-solving, and creativity in one field. As a student, I am currently focused on building a strong foundational understanding of programming concepts and computing principles. At university, my coursework exposes me to both theoretical concepts and practical applications. I am learning how websites are structured, how software is written, and how code can be used to solve problems. Each subject contributes to my understanding of how technology operates on a larger scale. Outside of my academic life, I have a deep interest in creative fields such as music, fashion, and makeup. These interests influence the way I think about design, colors, and self-expression, which also reflect in how I approach creative technical work. I see my personality as a balance between creativity and structure, and I hope to build a career that allows me to use both.

Career Objectives

Since I am still early in my academic journey, my career path is currently flexible. I am taking the time to explore different areas of technology to discover where my skills and interests are best suited. One of my key objectives is to work in a field that allows me to create something meaningful. Whether through software, websites, or future technologies, I want to be involved in building products that serve a purpose and bring value to others. I am particularly drawn to roles where I can combine technical thinking with creativity. Currently, I enjoy frontend development because it allows me to visually see the results of my work while strengthening my logical thinking skills. In the long term, I aim to gain experience in the workforce after completing my degree while continuing my education through advanced studies. I believe that combining academic knowledge with real-world experience will help me develop both professionally and personally.
